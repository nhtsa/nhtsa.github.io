VENNEX

ABOUT

Vennex is an American DJ duo consisting of Mike Huang and Ady Min. These two artists grew up in poor Atlanta, but managed to break the scene in 2012 with the release of the song "Bartlett Winter". Their unique Synthwave music attracted many fans. However, shortly after Mike Huang's son, Jeffrey, was diagnosed with terminal cancer, their music took a darker turn. Today, they remain one of the most prominent artists in the electronic genre
